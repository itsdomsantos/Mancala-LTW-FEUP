Instruções

Players take turns to play seeds. To take a turn, the player first chooses a non-empty hollow from one of the six in the near row and picks up all the seeds contained in it.

If a player cannot play because all six hollows are empty, the game ends and all the seeds on the other side of the board are captured by the other player. When one player has captured 25 seeds, the game ends and that player is the winner.

The game is drawn if both players accumulate 24 seeds or very occasionally in a stalemate situation when it becomes clear that the last remaining seeds are just being moved around the board with no chance of capturing.
