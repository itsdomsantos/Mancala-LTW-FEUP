Instructions

Players take turns to play seeds. To take a turn, the player first chooses a non-empty hollow in the near row and picks up all the seeds contained in it.

The seeds are spread to the other hollows and containers. If the last seed to be spread ends on the container, the player must play again. If the last seed to be spread ends on an empty hollow, the seeds from the opposite hollow will go to the container.

If a player cannot play because all hollows are empty, the game ends and all the seeds on the other side of the board are captured by the other player.
